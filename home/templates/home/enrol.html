Enrolment prerequisites

To enrol your child at Pinelands, you will need to provide evidence of you and your child's identity and proof of residence to ensure you reside within the school's catchment area.

To verify your identity and residence, you can use your driver's license or Australian passport. For your child, please provide a copy of his/her birth certificate.

To enrol, please print and complete the provided PDF form.

Enrolment submission

Enrolment forms must be submitted in-person or by post.

Once your enrolment has been submitted, Pinelands will contact you to complete the enrolment process.

*Enrolment fees apply.

Eligibility for enrolment

You are eligible to enrol if:

  • are an Australian citizen,
  • have lived in your current address for at least one month and,
  • your child is aged 10 years and over.
